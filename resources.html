School Rules and Policy 校规

  • Safety 安全
    • The school will provide a safe environment for our students
    • Parents are responsible for their own kids' safety and insurance coverage
    • CCLS is not responsible for any accident occurred to the students and for any items lost in the class
    • Space for the class activities: 9 classrooms, the small gym and hallways. Cafeteria and the commons area may be available

  • Notes to Parents 家长须知
    • Please participate the parent-on-duty work organized by PTO
    • Please drive safely through school zone. Watch out for children!
    • Please arrive at school at 1:30 p.m. so that you have ample time to find classrooms, gyms, cafeteria, restrooms, etc.
    • Please do not wear any types of shoes which can damage or leave marks on the gym floor
    • Please stay in the gym or cafeteria during the classes
    • If you would, please help volunteers during recess to keep the school in order
    • Please pick up your students and leave the school before 3:30 p.m.
  • Notes to Students 学生须知
    • No running in classrooms and hallway
    • No students allowed in areas not assigned to the Chinese School
    • No yelling or fighting
    • No drinks allowed in classrooms and gyms
    • No students allowed on the outside of the building
    • No damaging or stealing school or private property
    • No disobeying or verbally assaulting a teacher, staff member, or school employee
    • No putting down fellow classmates
  • Attendance 出席率
    • No drinks allowed in classrooms and gyms
    • Sign-in and out may be required
    • The class will be promptly started per a schedule
    • No students will be allowed to leave the school without the parent or the designated adult
    • The parent(s) need to pick up the student within 10 minutes after the class
    • 10 dollar childcare fee will billed to the parent(s) for every 10 minutes being late to pick up the student
  • Class Environment 班级环境
    • At the beginning of the class, students stand up and greet the teacher and the teacher greets back
    • Students need to raise their hand for questions
    • Talking, games, food, and drinks are not allowed during the class
  • Student Misbehavior 学生行为不端
    • Misbehaviour includes distracting other students in the class, using bad words, fighting, disturbing class order, etc.
    • Misbehaviour will be reported to parents and CCLS principal
    • Further action can be taken depending on the severity of the misbehaviour and the frequency
